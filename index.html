Welcome To LiF

LiF Consult PTY Limited is a Zambian and South African-registered company. LiF was established in Zambia in 2008 with a media arm producing LiF magazine. The company evolved to include LiF Consult in 2010, established primarily as an HR consulting firm. This has grown to include HR consulting, Leadership Development, Training and Executive coaching. The consulting firm was also registered in South Africa to offer the services listed above to Southern African countries (SADC).

Resourcing

Performance management

HR Policy Implementation

Organisational Transformation

Our Team

Eve Banda Nkumbula

Eve Banda Nkumbula is an Experienced Business leader with over 25 years of working closely with Chief Executives and C-Suite to build profitable organisations through Human Capital as a resource and providing leadership for successful Business turnaround. She has demonstrated success in working in the Telecoms, Mining Banking & Financial Services sectors across 15 African markets namely Zambia, Malawi, South Africa, Kenya, Ghana, Côte d’Ivoire, Mozambique, Mauritius, Angola, Democratic Republic of Congo, Zimbabwe, Nigeria, Botswana, Namibia, Lesotho Including Bahrain in the middle east and lived in 7 of those markets.

Eve holds a Bachelor of Arts, Development Studies and Public Administration from the University of Zambia conferred in 2000. Her postgraduate studies include an Advanced Management Development Programme from the London School of Business (2006), a Certified Consultant and Coach for Change programme from Oxford/HEC Paris (2009) and a Master’s Degree in Business Administration, from Kaplan University, USA conferred in 2015. In 2019, Eve was certified as a business coach by Gibs Institute under the University of Pretoria in South Africa.

Milimo Silenga - Zambian

Milimo is Human Resources and Culture Change Strategist with over 15 years of c-suite level, strategic business partnering and advisory experience in various sectors including, telecommunications, banking, insurance, aviation, and technology multinational organizations in various regions in Africa, Europe and North America. Her key achievements include successfully driving the HR and culture change strategies for the following companies; Zamtel following its acquisition by LapGreen, Atlas Mara during its acquisition of BancABC and merger with Finance Bank, as well as the Access Bank and Cavmont Bank merger and acquisition.

Milimo holds a Bachelor’s degree in Mass Communication and Public Administration from the University of Zambia, a Master’s degree from HEC and Oxford University in Coaching and Consulting for Change as well as a Post-graduate Diploma in Human Resources Management from Centennial College in Canada. She is also a certified Ken Blanchard Facilitator of the “Leading People Through Change” program.

Liesel Diesel - South African

Passionate about the link between business growth and engaged employees - this is what has motivated Liesl since she joined the world of Human Resources in 1986. Over the years, Liesl has contributed to the Retail, Manufacturing, Mining and Financial Services industries. During the course of her career, she has worked within Operational environments as well as at a senior Corporate level. She is also an entrepreneur. With South Africa as her beloved home and work base, Liesl has also worked in Botswana, Zambia, Namibia, Mozambique, Angola, Malawi as well as off-continent in the USA and UK. Areas of expertise and interest include Employee Engagement, Leadership Development, Learning and Development and Human Resources in general. With a proven ability to build relationships and partner with business regarding all things business results, people and talent – she brings insight and richness to her interactions and work.

Liesl completed a National Diploma in Personnel Management at the Port Elizabeth Technikon in 1986. In 2011, she completed the Barloworld Executive Development Programme at Wits Business School. This programme included studies and practical work in China

Ivy Anan Mawuko - Ghanaian

A passionate people development leader who heads the Learning and Development agenda for Africa and Offshore for one of the leading financial services organisations in Africa. Ivy also has over 24 years of work experience in different geographies in Africa, Europe and the United States.

Ivy is known for her solutionfocused approaches with her down-to-earth advisory support to organisations, leaders, and individuals to step into a place of growth and performance. Ivy has experience coaching leaders at all levels toward behavioural change and personal mastery. She has coached several executives and senior leadership teams across Africa. She is a gifted facilitator of processes such as team effectiveness, leadership conversations around organisational culture, performance, team alignment, etc. Ivy has a way of holding the space for meaningful dialogue to take place that results in making teams, individuals and organisations more effective. Ivy Anan Mawuko (Ghanaian) Life is beautiful Ivy holds a Bachelor of Arts from the University of Ghana and an MPhil from the University of Bergen, Norway.

Gbenebichie Oritsejolomi David - Africa

Gbenebichie Oritsejolomi David is the Founder and Executive Director of The Leadership Farm, a pioneering leadership institute established in Zambia in 2009. As an internationally recognized speaker, author, mentor, media personality, and educator, he specializes in Servant, Transformational, Authentic, Responsible (S.T.A.R.) leadership development with a singular vision: inspiring Africa’s transformation by cultivating its next generation of leaders.

A respected thought leader across East, West, and Southern Africa, Oritsejolomi’s expertise has been instrumental in establishing landmark leadership institutions, including the National Transformational Leadership Institute (NTLI) at the University of Juba, South Sudan, and The Africa Centre for Transformative and Inclusive Leadership (ACTIL) at Kenyatta University, Kenya.

His distinguished academic credentials include an Honorary Doctorate in Ministry (DMin), an LL.B from the University of Benin, qualification as a Barrister at the Nigerian Law School (BL), an MBA from Lancaster University (UK), and ongoing doctoral research in Innovative Urban Leadership at Bakke Graduate University (BGU), USA.
